Our Doctors

Dr. Nick Man Vu

Dr. Nick Man Vu is very passionate about high quality patient and holistic vision care. His philosophy is caring empathetic and easily accessible health care for everyone. He specializes in Pediatrics, Glaucoma, complex Contacts Lens fitting and Advance diseases with total treatment, management, and diagnosis of most major conditions. Being trilingual in Vietnamese, Spanish, and English allows Dr. Vu a more personalize approach and better relationship with his valued patients.

Dr. Vu has two bachelors degrees from the University of California, Irvine and from Pennsylvania College of Optometry (PCO). He has also published biological research papers at his Undergraduate University. He graduated with Honors in Pediatrics at the PCO and received the Doctor of Optometry Degree from the National Boards of Examiner.

On his days off, Dr. Vu enjoys relaxing and spending time with his wife Elisa, toddler son Chase and young daughter Olivia. His family,friends, and him love traveling to Disney World and building new enriching life experiences together. Other hobbies include Japanese gadening, ornamental koi fish, camping,fishing and working on outdoor projects.

Our doctors are available on Tuesday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday
