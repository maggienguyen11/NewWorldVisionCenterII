Our Mission

Since our grand opening on June 29, 2002, our primary mission is to enhance quality of life by improving sight, preventing disease progression, and advancing better eye health through superior compassionate vision care and outstanding customer satisfaction. We are committed to secured confidentiality and the very best accessible and affordable care for all patients.

Our Passion

At New World Vision Center II, we strive our best to deliver each of our patient a customized comprehensive quality vision eye care experience, uncompromising excellent contact lens fitting with matching follow up lens comfort reviews and evaluations, glaucoma and disease management, and major pre- and post-operative procedures. Our CPO (Certified Professional Associate) Staffs and our ABOC Licensed Opticians, together all share our primary mission to provide only the most precise and individualized balance ophthalmic corrections possible.
